- **3GB**: Can handle up to 5-8% commission  
- **4GB+**: Can only handle 1-4% commission safely
- **10GB+**: Maximum 1-2% commission to stay profitable

### **The Problem with Your Original Tiers:**
- **Your original**: ‚Çµ400+/day = 5% commission
- **Reality**: Most packages break even at 1-4% commission
- **Solution**: Much higher daily thresholds + lower commission rates

## üéØ **RECOMMENDED TIER STRUCTURE**

### **New Tier System - Higher Thresholds, Lower Commission Rates:**

| Tier | Daily Order Value | Commission Rate | Monthly Equivalent | Target Agent Profile |
|------|-------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Basic** | ‚Çµ0-999/day | **0%** | ‚Çµ0-30,000/month | New/casual agents |
| **Bronze** | ‚Çµ1,000-2,999/day | **1%** | ‚Çµ30,000-90,000/month | Part-time agents |
| **Silver** | ‚Çµ3,000-6,999/day | **1.5%** | ‚Çµ90,000-210,000/month | Active agents |
| **Gold** | ‚Çµ7,000-14,999/day | **2%** | ‚Çµ210,000-450,000/month | Business agents |
| **Platinum** | ‚Çµ15,000+/day | **2.5%** | ‚Çµ450,000+/month | Power agents |

### **Why These Tiers Work:**

1. **Basic Tier (0% commission):**
   - No commission expense for you
   - Agents still get wholesale pricing (good value)
   - Encourages growth to earn commissions

2. **Bronze Tier (‚Çµ1,000+/day, 1% commission):**
   - Only serious agents reach this level
   - 1% commission is safe for almost all packages
   - ‚Çµ1,000/day = 50-250 packages depending on mix

3. **Higher Tiers (1.5%-2.5%):**
   - Very high daily thresholds ensure only power agents qualify
   - Even 2.5% commission keeps most packages profitable
   - These agents generate massive volume for you
